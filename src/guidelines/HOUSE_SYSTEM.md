## How Houses Work

### Joining a House (First Time)
1. Navigate to the Houses/Guilds page
2. Browse available houses and their current rankings
3. Click "Join House" on your chosen house
4. Confirm in the join dialog
5. Immediately receive +100 XP bonus and +1.1x XP multiplier

### Switching Houses (Limited to 3 Switches)
**Important:** House switching has strategic limits!

**Before November 20th:**
- You CAN switch houses up to **3 times total**
- Click "Switch House" on any other house
- Confirm in the switch dialog (shows switches remaining)
- Your XP multiplier continues (+1.1x)
- All your earned XP stays with you
- Your contribution moves from old house to new house
- Each switch increments your switch counter (max 3)
- "Switches Left" displayed on Houses page

**After November 20th (Lockout):**
- All house switching is LOCKED
- You cannot switch regardless of switches used
- "Switch House" button shows error message
- You remain in your current house until Season 1 ends
- Season end rewards based on house you're in on November 30th

### Leaving a House (Not Recommended)
1. Click "Leave House" from your current house card
2. Confirm in the dialog (warning about losing benefits)
3. XP multiplier is removed immediately
4. Rejoining a different house counts as one of your 3 switches
5. Cannot switch at all after November 20th

## House Rankings

### How Houses Are Ranked
Houses are ranked by **Total House XP**, which is the sum of all XP earned by all house members during the season.

### Leaderboard Metrics
- **Members**: Total number of members in the house
- **Total XP**: Combined XP of all house members
- **Avg XP**: Average XP per member (Total XP / Members)

### Real-time Updates
- Rankings update in real-time as members earn XP
- Your contributions directly impact your house's position
- More active members = higher total XP

## Strategic Considerations

### Choosing a House

**Joining a Leading House:**
- ✅ Higher chance of season-end rewards
- ✅ Larger, more active community
- ❌ Your individual impact is smaller
- ❌ May be harder to rise through ranks

**Joining an Underdog House:**
- ✅ Your contributions have more impact
- ✅ Potential for leadership positions
- ✅ Closer-knit community
- ❌ Lower chance of top 3 finish
- ❌ Fewer active members for collaboration

### Maximizing House Contributions
1. **Stay Active**: Daily logins maintain your streak and XP multipliers
2. **Complete Quests**: Focus on high-XP daily and weekly quests
3. **Make Predictions**: Each prediction earns XP for you and your house
4. **Win Bets**: Winning bets provide significant XP boosts
5. **Maintain Streaks**: Long login streaks provide multiplier bonuses
6. **Upgrade Subscription**: Pro (1.5x) and Elite (2.0x) multiply your XP contributions

## XP Multiplier Stacking

House multipliers stack with other bonuses:

**Example Calculation:**
- Base action: Make a prediction = 25 XP
- Elite subscription: 2.0x
- 7-day streak: 1.2x
- House member: 1.1x
- **Total multiplier**: 2.0 × 1.2 × 1.1 = 2.64x
- **Final XP**: 25 × 2.64 = **66 XP** (vs 25 XP without bonuses)

All this XP counts toward:
1. Your personal level progression
2. Your house's total XP
3. Your Season 1 total (for end-of-season bonus)

## Season End Process

### What Happens on November 30, 2025 at 11:59 PM

1. **Rankings Freeze**: Final house rankings are locked
2. **Season Totals Calculated**: Each user's Season 1 XP is tallied
3. **Multipliers Applied**: 
   - 1st place house members: Season XP × 3
   - 2nd place house members: Season XP × 2
   - 3rd place house members: Season XP × 1.5
4. **Bonus XP Awarded**: Massive XP bonuses applied to all accounts
5. **Level Ups**: Many users will level up multiple times
6. **Platform Recognition**: Top houses receive eternal bragging rights and special badges
7. **$DEOR Distribution**: 1st place house gets 5% of $DEOR allocation
